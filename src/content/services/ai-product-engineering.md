Most AI products fail because they focus on the technology instead of the user workflow.

Users do not care which model powers the application.

They care whether the product:
- saves time
- improves workflows
- reduces friction
- creates meaningful value

That is why the focus is not just on AI integration itself, but on building scalable systems where AI improves the overall product experience naturally.
